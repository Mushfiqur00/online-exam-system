Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I cannot log into my account. What should I do?
A: Ensure you are using the correct credentials and selecting the right role (Student vs Admin) on the login page. Students can reset passwords by contacting their institutional Admin.
Q: How do institutional Admins create exam groups?
A: Admins can navigate to "Group Management" in their dashboard, click "Create Group", provide a name, and a unique Group Code will be generated.
Q: Is student progress saved if the exam disconnects?
A: Yes, Examora auto-saves submissions periodically. If disconnected, you can reconnect and resume within the allowed exam time window if the Admin permits.
